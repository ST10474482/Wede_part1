Our Story

Belem Bakery was established in the 1970s and has since expanded from a modest family-run business to become one of Johannesburg's most well-known Portuguese bakeries. Each item is made by hand using age-old recipes that have been handed down through the generations.

John Lourenço became a silent partner in 2001, and Shaun De Carvalho contributed his skills in hospitality to the group in the 2010s. The Belém Café and Restaurant in Glenanda opened as a result, and catering and wholesale services were added.

Mission

By providing freshly baked breads, pastries, and savory treats made with real recipes and premium ingredients, we hope to celebrate and preserve the rich traditions of Portuguese baking.

Vision

To be viewed as the best Portuguese bakery and café in South Africa, renowned for its genuineness, artistry, and strong ties to the local community and culture.

Meet the Team

From our pastry chefs and master bakers to our café employees and catering coordinators, Belem's success is the result of a dedicated team that values quality and genuineness.
