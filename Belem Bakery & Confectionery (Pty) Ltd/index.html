Welcome to Belem Bakery

Here in Johannesburg, you can find genuine Portuguese baked goods.

Portuguese warmth, flavor, and tradition are brought to your table every day with our freshly made, handcrafted treats, which range from golden pastéis de nata to crispy prawn rissoles.

  • Freshly baked daily using traditional recipes
  • Wide selection of sweet and savory pastries
  • Catering & dine-in at our Regents Park Café
  • Serving Johannesburg since 1978
